Mahamandir Temple

Jodhpur , Rajasthan

Description

Located on the Mandore Road, the Maha Mandir temple at Jodhpur is a splendid piece of architecture, It is truly a treat to the eye. The popularity of this particular temple has skyrocketed so much in the last few years that it now enjoys a status of being at the center of Rajasthan Tourist Attractions. The temple boasts a beautiful design that features various positions of Yoga. The best part of these various design attributes is the detailing. With a detailed detailing the beauty of the temple often makes tourists awestruck. The temple has a unique appeal for the tourists as it is located in the midst of a peaceful surrounding and provides a calm ambiance to the visitors. However, if you are not really tired as such then visiting the Maha Mandir is going to be an awesome experience as there are a number of tourists attraction in close proximity to the temple. Presence of a number of entertainment centers with renowned restaurants will also help you to have taste of local cuisines. Moreover, if you fall in love with the place, then there are a number of quality accommodation facilities in the surrounding area to help you nest in this beautiful location.

Best Time To Visit Mahamandir

If you are thinking about visiting Mahamandir Jodhpur, then let us tell you that although you can visit Maha Mandir at any time of the year, but if you want to visit Maha Mandir as well as other tourist places of Jodhpur. If you are planning, then let us tell you that the best time to visit Jodhpur is from October to March. Winter season is a favorable time to visit Udaipur. Avoid traveling to Jodhpur during summers starting from March as the temperature of Jodhpur reaches up to 45 degree Celsius during this time which may discourage your visit to Jodhpur.

History of Temple

Built in 1812 and standing as one of the prime attractions of the city of Jodhpur is the Mahamandir Temple. It is well known for its marvellous piece of architecture that stands on 84 beautifully carved pillars. These pillars display intricate stone carvings that depict yoga postures. The walls of the temple are designed with traditional motifs as well that have an amazing royal aura about itself. Mahamandir Temple is supported by 84 pillars and ornamented with detailed designs and figures depicting various postures of Yoga. Located at a distance of 2 km from Jodhpur on the road towards Mandore, the temple was built in 1812. In the premises of the temple, there are many ancient shrines and houses that reflect intricate works of stone. Traditional motifs act as the stud to the walls and pillars of the temple

How To Reach Mahamandir

If you are planning to go to Mahamandir Jodhpur by Airplane, then let us tell you that the nearest airport to Mahamandir is its domestic Jodhpur Airport which is located about 08 kms away from Mahamandir. This airport is connected by air to many major cities of the country like Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur and Kolkata. After landing at the airport, you can hire a cab or book a pre-paid taxi to reach Mahamandir Jodhpur easily. Traveling by road to Mahamandir Jodhpur can prove to be quite comfortable as the city is well connected by road network to many major cities of India like Mumbai, Delhi, Indore, Kota and Ahmedabad. There are many options in front of you to travel by bus. You can travel to the Mahamandir via deluxe buses, AC coaches and state-run buses. If you have chosen the rail route to reach Jodhpur, then let us tell you that Jodhpur is a major city of Rajasthan which is situated on a vast network of rail. Jodhpur railway station is well connected by trains to major cities of India such as Jaipur, Delhi, Kolkata, Indore, Mumbai and Kota. Several trains run daily from many major cities of India to Jodhpur. After reaching the railway station, you can hire a taxi, cab or an auto-rickshaw to reach Mahamandir Jodhpur.

Opening and Closing Timings

The Mahamandir is open for devotees daily from 5.00 am to 12.00 noon and from 4.00 pm to 9.00 pm. And for your information, please let us know that you must take 1-2 hours for a pleasant and joyful visit to the Mahamandir.

Architecture of Temple

Mahamandir Temple is supported by 84 pillars which are adorned with intricate stone carvings exhibiting yogic postures, frescoes and other artwork within its premises. The main deity inside the temple is of Lord Shiva. Located around 2 km away from Jodhpur on the road to Mandore, Mahamandir boasts of various pre-historic shrines and houses adorned with brilliant stone works. The walls of the temple are studded with ancient motifs which enhance the beauty of the huge temple. The architectural sculptures of the temple are quite incomparable. It depicts the skilled craftsmanship of the people of Rajasthan.
