Phool Mahal

Jodhpur , Rajasthan

Description

Phool Mahal, Jodhpur is also called the palace of flowers. This hall is the most lavish in the Mehrangarh Fort. This palace was built in the 18th century and it was used for entertainment by the Maharajas. Female dancers were invited to entertain the Maharajas on special occasions. This palace is less accessible to the people as compared to other parts of the fort. You will be astonished by the extravagant interior of Phool Mahal, Jodhpur as every inch of this palace screams royalty.

History of Phool Mahal, Jodhpur

The history of this palace is quite interesting. The Phool Mahal, Jodhpur was built by Maharaj Abhaya Singh, who ruled the region from 1724 to 1749. The interior of the palace resembles stunning flowers, and the ceiling of the palace has a golden filigree work. It is said that in the olden days, the gold was brought from Ahmedabad and Gujarat to carry out the detailing of this palace’s interior. You will also find numerous paintings and portraits. The furniture in the Phool Mahal is from the British period to give the interior of this place a royal finish.

Architecture

The Phool Mahal is a beautifully decorated hall in the Mehrangarh fort, Jodhpur. The hall probably served the purpose of a pleasure dome of the Maharajas, and the female dancers used to stay here. Accordingly the room was made to look as beautiful as a magnificent flower, which earned it the name Phool mahal. The ceiling has golden filigree work, for which it is said, gold was brought from Ahmedabad, Gujarat. There are also some beautiful portraits and paintings, especially the raga mala series, and furniture from the British period to enhance the glory of the room.

How to reach Phool palace

Jodhpur Airport is the Nearest Airport which is 3 km away.Jodhpur Railway Station is the Nearest Airport which is 2.7 km away.Jodhpur Bus Station is 2 km away; also Jodhpur is well connected with other major cities

Entry fee

The entry fee for Indians is rupees 20 and for foreigners, rupees 250 each.

Best time to visit

From November to middle of February will be the best time to visit this place, because, during summer and monsoon conditions become unbearable.

Important Travel Tips

To explore the Phool Mahal and some of its surrounding attractions comfortably, you need to take care of certain things while traveling. Here are some of the travel tips to help you get through your trip easily. • Wear comfortable shoes if you are planning to cover more than one location at a stretch • Don’t forget to carry your hat and sunglasses if you are visiting during the day to stay protected from the sun. • Carry your bottle of water if you are planning to visit the Mehrangarh Fort and other surrounding attractions. • Cameras are allowed inside the fort, so click a lot of pictures to make memories. • There are many guides available, and you can hire them at affordable rates. • You can also explore the nearby monument that is the Jaswant Thada along with Mehrangarh Fort because they both are a location in very close distance.
